# Privacy Policy for LegacyLoop

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Profile Information:** Email, username, bio, and college details.
- **Content:** Memories (posts), photos, and interactions (likes, comments).
- **Metadata:** IP addresses and timestamps for security audit purposes.

## 2. How We Use Information
- To provide the social "Loop" experience.
- To secure your account and prevent unauthorized access.
- To improve the service dynamics.

## 3. Data Control
- You have the right to edit your profile at any time.
- You can delete your posts, which removes them and associated media from our primary storage.
- Private posts are only visible to you.

## 4. Security
- We use industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit.
- Row Level Security (RLS) ensures that only you can modify your data.
